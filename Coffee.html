What makes our coffee special

The first thing comes to mind when thinking about specialty
coffee is its advanced quality. The flavours hidden in a cup of coffee
are rich and crisp and comes not just from the quality of the 
specialty coffee beans, but from the work ethic and dedication put
into it by the framers.


speciality coffee is defined as any coffee that scores above 80
points on a 100 points scale.Typically, specialty coffee is grown at
high attitudes,with mush care and attention from the framer. From
there it is sold at a premium to coffee traders or direct to roasters.
